Welcome to my another life as a photographer!

A photographer is a secret about a seret .The more it tells you ,the less you know.


—— Diane Arbus

—— Everyone has a secret base in their hearts ，where they can accept their truest selves.
—— For me, my secret base contains my thoughts about the world and humanity, my thoughts about myself. Photography forms part of the secret base, allowing me to think outside the ordinary vision and see the world from a new perspective.
—— So actually, you can experience my observation of the world through my work, and then guess who I am, after all, the best way to understand a person is to look at her work.
